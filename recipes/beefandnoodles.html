Beefy Noodles

Noodles with shredded beef

What's more comforting than a warm bowl of noodles with slow cooker beef? Have dinner ready in a snap with this low-effort recipe

Ingredients

  • Worcestershire sauce
  • 1lb chuck roast
  • Minced garlic
  • 1 package of wide egg noodles
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Salt chuck roast generously and let sit overnight in fridge
  2. Remove roast and insert into slow cooker. Top with minced garlic and Worcestershire sauce
  3. Let cook for 4-8 hours
  4. When finished, shred with fork
  5. Prepare noodles according to package
  6. Top noodles with beef and enjoy!
