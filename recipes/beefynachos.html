Beefy Nachos with Raw Cheddar

Beef nachos on a skillet

Tired of overthinking dinner? Overthink no more with this easy peasy beefy nachos made in 15 minutes or less.

Ingredients

  • 6-8 oz of raw cheddar
  • 1lb of ground beef
  • 1 package of Siete tortilla chips
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 275 degrees
  2. Cook ground beef and season with salt to taste
  3. Lay out chips over pan and grate cheese until desired amount
  4. Insert chips and cheese into oven for 10 minutes or until melted
  5. Top with beef and enjoy!
